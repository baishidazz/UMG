Our Story

DSP began with a simple idea: retail-simple, pro-powerful. After a private beta in Q2 2025, we launched in July 2025 with a latency-optimized engine and a compliance-first posture. Our principles—Trust, Clarity, Discipline—guide every release.

Q2 2025 — Private Beta
Core engine, custody, risk controls.
July 2025 — Public Launch
Light + Dark UI, proof-of-reserves roadmap.
H2 2025 — API v2
Granular rate limits, sandbox upgrades.

Principles

  • Trust — transparent fees, security disclosures, external audits.
  • Clarity — human-readable docs, clean UI, explicit risk notices.
  • Discipline — ship small, verify, improve. No hype.

Security by Design
