常见问题

Frequently Asked Questions

关于我们的音乐赚钱平台的常见问题解答

Find answers to common questions about our music earning platform

我们的平台与音乐艺术家和广告商合作。当您听音乐时，您正在帮助推广这些艺术家和品牌。广告收入的一部分作为收益与您分享。您听的越多（在每日限制内），您赚的越多。
Our platform partners with music artists and advertisers. When you listen to music, you're helping promote these artists and brands. A portion of the advertising revenue is shared with you as earnings. The more you listen (up to daily limits), the more you earn.
当您的余额达到最低提现金额10美元时，您可以随时提现。提现将在1-3个工作日内处理。VIP会员享有更快的提现处理时间。
You can withdraw your earnings anytime your balance reaches the minimum withdrawal amount of $10. Withdrawals are processed within 1-3 business days. VIP members enjoy faster withdrawal processing times.
您的每日收益受VIP等级限制。VIP等级越高，每日收益限制越高。没有终身限制 - 通过持续使用平台，您可以随着时间的推移赚取任意金额。
Your daily earnings are limited by your VIP level. Higher VIP levels have higher daily earning limits. There's no lifetime limit - you can earn as much as you want over time by consistently using the platform.
是的，您可以从多个设备访问您的账户，但您一次只能在一个设备上主动听音乐。我们的系统会检测并防止来自不同设备的同步收听。
Yes, you can access your account from multiple devices, but you can only be actively listening on one device at a time. Our system detects and prevents simultaneous listening from different devices.
