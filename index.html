Common questions about Universal Music

Universal Music partners with artists to generate revenue through advertising displayed during music playback. We share most of this revenue (70%) with our users. Simply choose your VIP level and listen to music daily to earn proportional income.

Earnings are calculated per minute, with different rates for each VIP level (e.g., 1%-3% for VIP1, 4%-6% for VIP4). Earnings are settled daily and can be withdrawn once you reach $100, with processing completed within 1-3 business days.

VIP fees are primarily used to acquire higher quality music licenses and advertising resources, ensuring you receive stable earnings. Higher VIP levels allow us to secure better advertising resources for you, resulting in higher earning rates.

As a music industry leader, all advertising impressions and earnings data are audited by third parties. You can view detailed earnings reports in your account, including playback duration and corresponding earnings for each song.
